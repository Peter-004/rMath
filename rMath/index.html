About rMaths

rMaths is a free, open-source project pioneered by Peter Adebayo. Designed for enthusiasts of Mathematics and Computer Science, rMaths bridges the gap between these two fields, enabling users to leverage the power of computational thinking to solve complex mathematical challenges. By integrating principles of data structures, algorithms, and computer science, rMaths provides innovative solutions to a variety of mathematical problems, making it an invaluable tool for students, researchers, and professionals alike
